## Summary
Validated data summary (Long Term stability version) for study SS25071BV02 (Measurement of FB7013 antisense and sense strands in Cynomolgus Monkey Plasma). Contains comprehensive data tables for the validation of the LC-MS/MS method, including sensitivity, selectivity, specificity, matrix effect, accuracy & precision, recovery, dilution integrity, and extensive stability data (Short-term, Freeze-thaw, Post-preparative, Long-term up to 101/103 days, and Whole blood stability).
